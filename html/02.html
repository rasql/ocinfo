HTML Tutorial

Handling of white space

In general text, white space (spaces, tabs and line returns) are all ignored and replaced with one single space character.

In order to apply simple styles to text (such as bold, italics and underline) the text is surrounded with tags. Each opening tag has a corresponding closing tag.

Headings

In order to indicate the importance of titels, up to 6 levels can be selected for headings by using the tags h1 to h1.

Ordered lists

Ordered (or numbered) lists are surrounded by the ol tag. Each list item is surrounded by the il tag.

Un-ordered lists

Un-ordered (or bullet) lists are surrounded by the ul tag. Each list item is surrounded by the il tag.
